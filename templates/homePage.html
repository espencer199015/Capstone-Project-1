Dream Ride Stables


About the Farm

Dream Ride Stables is a 200-acre farm with 30 lesson horses.
We have 4 fantastic riding instructors!
Ms. Roxanne and Ms. Pat are here for all our beginner riders of all ages.
Ms. Jay and Mr. Trent are here for all riders ready to take their riding to the next level and out into the show ring.
This farm offers hunt-seat and western riding for all levels of riders.
The facilities include 2 barns, 2 indoor rings, and 4 outdoor rings.
The farm also provides 50 acres of wooded trails and hill work.
We offer trail riding groups on specified weekend dates.
Check the website often for farm and riding event updates.
Happy trails from Dream Ride Stables!
